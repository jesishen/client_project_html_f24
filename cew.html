We are the Center for the Education of Women +

You belong here!

At CEW+, we help you overcome challenges by offering academic, financial, and professional support to help you achieve your full potential. Originally created to support women in higher education, we now uplift all underserved communities at U-M and beyond. Through career and education counseling, financial aid, workshops, events, and a welcoming, diverse community, we’re dedicated to empowering you. We are CEW+, and we’re here to help you succeed.
