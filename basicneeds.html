Basic Needs

Who Are We?

We strive for fair and equitable access to food and other basic resources

The Basic Needs website is designed for University of Michigan students who may be facing challenges related to food, housing, and financial security. It serves as a central resource for students seeking assistance with essential needs, offering information on campus and community services that provide support. Whether you’re dealing with food insecurity, housing instability, or financial stress, the Basic Needs website connects you to the resources and guidance needed to help you thrive both academically and personally at U-M.

Our Key Resources:

  • Critical Incident Support & Conflict Management: Receive support if you’re affected by conflicts or critical incidents.
  • Financial Assistance: We offer resources to address immediate and long-term financial needs.
  • Food Resources: Find support for maintaining healthy eating habits and accessing food resources.
  • Health Care Support: Get help with medical, dental, and mental health needs.
  • Housing and Transportation: Access resources for housing and transportation challenges.

Don't hesitate to contact us if you need any of these resources!
